Peanuts

Peanuts is a Sunday American comic strip, written and illustrated by Charles M. Schulz. It ran from October 2, 1950 - Feburary 13, 2000. It remains one of the more influential and Widely read comic strips in history. The strip is known The Peanuts characters, created by Charles M. Schulz, are iconic figures in American popular culture.

Charlie Brown

He is the main character of the Peanuts franchise. He is a loveable, kind hearted, and often helpless boy who faces many challenges and failures but never gives up! He is known for his iconic Zig Zag shirt and loop of hair on his head.
